Overview:

In today's digital world, big data is big business. Yet, current data mining technologies offer limited performance due to high response times and the need for guesswork to determine optimal parameter settings for outlier exploration. These impediments can cause delays that put analysts at competitive disadvantages when anormalty need to be discovered quickly. The ONION tools represent a leap forward that makes interactive outlier analytic feasible and pratical by offereing super quick outlier detection and insight into outlierness among data points. By pre-processing dataset and extracting the relationship between outlierness and parameter settings, ONION enable anlysts to discovered outliers in real time, exploring large datasets at the speed of thought.

Advantages:
